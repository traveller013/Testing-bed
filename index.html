Main Contributor

The main contributor to the Edsinger Gallery, the titular Daryian Edsinger was born in Orgeon. Growing up she dreamed of stealing this car and in the summer of 2016 she gathered a crew and pulled of the crime of the year. Posing as a visiting photographer she infiltrated the museum and distraced the curator. Meanwhile the Hackman spliced into the security cameras and caused a fatal error. Later, the Wrench Monkey and the Actor pose as contractors sent to replace the cameras. By, 0236 the crew drove off with the classic car of her dreams.

Dariyan Edsinger now spends her time plotting the downfall of her neighbors and taking care of her overly energtic ferrets.
